Autoship Plan® - Scheduling Your Orders
Autoship Plan® is the Tiens scheduled delivery program. Simply order what you want once and the products you have specified will be shipped to your delivery address at the delivery interval you have pre-selected. You may change your orders at any time, including the products, shipping address and delivery interval. You may create more than 1(one) Autoship Plan®.
